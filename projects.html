S.O.R.N (Vertical Slice)

S.O.R.N is a fast-paced mech shooter set in a massive desert and monlith scale mech facility. As a level designer, I was responsible for designing Directive 1, our first mission and level in this game.

Bootlegger Bust

Bootlegger Bust is an FPS level set in a Prohibition-era warehouse used as a mafia operation hub. As a level designer, I was responsible for designing the layout, enemy placement, and pacing of this level.

Carnage

Carnage is a high-octane, Burnout 3 inspired mobile game. As a level designer, I was responsible for designing the core gameplay loop and creating challenging, dynamic tracks.

About Me

Hello, I am Paul Saturno, a level designer focused on creating immersive and engaging gameplay experiences. I enjoy building levels that feel intuitive, encourage exploration, and keep players invested from start to finish.

Currently, I am a senior at the University of Central Florida and will graduate in Spring 2026 with a bachelor’s degree in Digital Media – Video Game Design. I previously earned a bachelor’s in Business Administration from UCF in 2022. Together, these degrees allow me to approach game design from both a creative and strategic perspective.
